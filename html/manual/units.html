Beam parameters

The beam parameters are defined in core.beam_parameters and stored in the BeamParameters object. We use the same units and conventions as TraceWin.

Note

Envelopes are at \(1\sigma\) in LightWin, while they are plotted at \(6\sigma\) by default in TraceWin.

Note

Envelopes are calculated with un-normalized emittances in the \([z-\delta]\) and \([z-z']\) planes, but they are calculated with normalized emittance in the \([\phi-W]\) plane.

\(\sigma\) beam matrix

We save this matrix in SI units, i.e. in \(\mathrm{m}\) and \(\mathrm{rad}\). When given in the .ini configuration file, it must also be in SI units.
